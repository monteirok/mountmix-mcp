# MountMix Cocktail Program Overview

## Mission
- **Statement:** Elevate celebrations in the Canadian Rockies by offering premium, artful cocktail experiences that combine local ingredients, expert technique, and stunning mountain ambiance.
- **Core Values:**
  - High quality & craftsmanship
  - Local and regional authenticity
  - Memorable guest experience
  - Flexibility and customization
- **Emotional Appeal:** Deliver more than drinks—create mountain-flavoured moments, wow-factor ambiance, and elevated atmospheres for every event.

## Content Strategy
- **Hero Tagline:** "Elevated Cocktails, Mountain Views" to immediately signal premium service grounded in place.
- **About / Story:** Highlight Canmore and the broader Canadian Rockies roots, passion for premium ingredients, and dedication to cocktail artistry.
- **Services:** Detailed pages for weddings, corporate events, private parties, destination gatherings, and full bar service.
- **Signature Menu:** Feature cocktails such as Rocky Mountain Old Fashioned, Alpine Gin Garden, Summit Punch, and Campfire Sour with pricing transparency ($13–$17).
- **Gallery:** Showcase event setups, mountain backdrops, and cocktail presentations to help clients visualize their own celebration.
- **Testimonials:** Include authentic client quotes with event type and location for social proof.
- **Contact / CTA:** Prominent "Get My Custom Quote" form capturing event type, guest count, budget, vision, and location.
- **Why Choose Us / Credentials:** Emphasize licensing, insurance, local sourcing, professional equipment, and menu customization capabilities.
- **Service Area & Logistics:** Communicate coverage of Canmore, Banff, Calgary, and the surrounding Rocky Mountain region plus response expectations.

## Product & Packages
- **Core Offering:** Premium craft cocktail catering delivered on-site at client venues.
- **Primary Packages:**
  - Wedding cocktail service
  - Corporate and gala events
  - Private parties and intimate gatherings
  - Destination events (mountain lodges, ski chalets, outdoor venues)
  - Full bar service encompassing wine, beer, spirits, and signature cocktails
- **Customization & Upsells:**
  - Tailored menu design aligned with themes or brand identities
  - Premium glassware, presentation, and professional equipment upgrades
  - Specialty ingredients, garnishes, and bespoke cocktail development
  - Scalable staffing to match guest count and event pace
- **Pricing Transparency:** Sample cocktail pricing anchors expectations ($13–$17 per cocktail).
- **Operational Logistics:** Licensed and insured team, mobile setups for remote venues, and contingency planning for mountain environments.

## Opportunities & Recommendations
- **Clarify Package Tiers:** Introduce clearly named tiers (e.g., Summit, Alpine, Ridge) or Bronze/Silver/Gold levels to simplify decision-making.
- **Deepen Differentiation:** Spotlight local ingredient sourcing partners and tell the story behind each signature drink to emphasize regional inspiration.
- **Strengthen Lead Capture:** Offer downloadable assets such as a sample menu or cocktail planning guide as a lead magnet in addition to the quote form.
- **Reduce Perceived Risk:** Share logistics playbooks—setup timelines, remote venue handling, and weather contingencies—to reassure clients.
